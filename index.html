About John Roofers

of Roofing Excellence in Houston
Three Generations

Roofing runs deep in my family. My grandfather laid his first shingle in Houston decades ago, and my dad carried on that proud tradition. Growing up, I watched them both build lasting relationships with homeowners across Texas, one quality roof at a time. What struck me wasn't just their craftsmanship—it was their integrity and commitment to doing things right.

When I started John Roofers in 2010, I saw a real need in Houston for honest, skilled roofers who treat every project like it's their own home. Too many homeowners were getting burned by fly-by-night contractors with hidden fees and shoddy work. I knew there had to be a better way—the way my grandfather and father taught me.

Today, we bring that same three-generation expertise to every roof replacement, new construction project, and home extension in Houston and beyond. Our team combines traditional craftsmanship with modern roofing technology, and we back it all with transparent pricing and owner-operated accountability. Whether you're in Houston, Austin, or Dallas, you're getting a roofing company that still believes in doing business the right way.

My grandfather taught me that a roof isn't just shingles and nails—it's a promise to protect what matters most. That's the standard we uphold on every single project.
