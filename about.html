Our Story

Driven by Quality & Integrity
Built on Tradition

Roofing runs deep in my family. My grandfather laid his first shingle in Houston decades ago, building a reputation for honest work and exceptional craftsmanship. My dad carried on that proud tradition, and I grew up watching both of them treat every customer's home like their own. What struck me wasn't just their skill with a hammer—it was their unwavering integrity and commitment to doing things right, even when no one was watching.

When I started John Roofers in 2010, I saw too many Houston homeowners frustrated by fly-by-night contractors who promised the world but delivered shoddy work and surprise charges. There weren't enough good roofers out there who operated with transparency and accountability. I knew I could make a difference by applying the principles my grandfather and father taught me: treat people fairly, stand behind your work, and never compromise on quality.

Today, John Roofers serves Houston, Austin, Dallas, and surrounding Texas communities with the same family values that guided my grandfather's work. We're still owner-operated, which means I personally ensure every project meets our high standards. Whether it's a roof replacement, new construction, or seamless extension roofing, we approach each job with the pride and precision that comes from three generations of roofing expertise. Our 4.8-star rating from over 50 reviews reflects our commitment to excellence, but it's the relationships we build with homeowners that truly define who we are.

My grandfather taught me that a roof isn't just shingles and nails—it's a promise to protect what matters most. That's the standard we uphold on every single project.
